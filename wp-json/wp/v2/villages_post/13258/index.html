      What is ThreatGEN\u00ae Red vs. Blue?<\/span><\/h1>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n
      \n
      \n
      \n

      ThreatGEN\u00ae Red vs. Blue is the next evolution in cybersecurity education, training, and IR tabletop exercises<\/strong>. It is a game-based cybersecurity simulation platform that combines the power of an actual computer gaming engine and adaptive adversary simulation A.I.<\/a><\/strong>, to provide the most practical and effective way for ANYONE<\/em> to learn cybersecurity, from beginners to experts, and even leadership. It doesn’t require any prior technical knowledge or command line skills… not even to play as the red team! Used for cybersecurity education, awareness, training, and IR tabletop exercises, ThreatGEN\u00ae Red vs. Blue is immersive, interactive, and hands-on, and it’s supported by an education portal with online courses, labs, scenarios, and an online community.<\/p>\n
